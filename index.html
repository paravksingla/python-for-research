Why This Book

I love Python and what it can do. While there is an abundance of literature on Python, I found very little sources of information where how you can use Python for your research tasks was documented.

So I decided to collect and compile everything in one place and present it in a nice format. There is a HarvardX course on this topic. Though its a little light on content, it is good as an introduction. You might also want to check that out.
